Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fully Homomorphic Encryption?

Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) is a form of encryption that allows computations to be performed on encrypted data without requiring access to a private key. Even though the data is encrypted, FHE enables our systems to analyze it and provide accurate predictions without ever seeing your actual health information.

Is my health data really secure?

Yes, your health data is encrypted end-to-end. With our FHE technology, your data remains encrypted even during analysis. Our system is designed so that not even our own servers can access your unencrypted health information.

What kind of health predictions can I receive?

Our system can provide predictions and insights related to various health metrics, risk assessments, and personalized health recommendations based on the data you provide. The predictions are generated using advanced machine learning models while maintaining complete data privacy.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our prediction models are continuously trained and refined using anonymized, aggregated data. While no prediction system is 100% accurate, our FHE approach allows us to leverage sophisticated algorithms while maintaining privacy, resulting in highly reliable insights comparable to traditional non-encrypted systems.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this platform?

Not at all. We've designed our platform to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. The complex encryption technology works behind the scenes, providing you with a simple, intuitive interface for inputting your data and receiving predictions.
