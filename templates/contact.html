Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fully Homomorphic Encryption?

FHE is an encryption technology that allows computations on encrypted data without decrypting it first, ensuring your health information remains private throughout the analysis process.

Is my health data really secure?

Yes, with our FHE technology, your health data remains encrypted end-to-end. Even our own systems cannot access your unencrypted information.

How do I get started?

Simply create an account, and you'll be guided through the process of securely inputting your health data for analysis.
