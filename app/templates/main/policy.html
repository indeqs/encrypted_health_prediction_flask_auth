Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

  • Profile information (such as demographic information)

2.2 Health Data

Our Service collects health-related information that you choose to provide, such as:

2.3 Usage Information

  • Log information (such as IP address, access times)

3. Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)

Our platform utilizes Fully Homomorphic Encryption, an advanced encryption technology that allows us to perform computations on encrypted data without having access to the underlying plaintext information.

When you submit health data to our Service:

  • Your data is encrypted on your device before transmission
  • The data remains encrypted while stored on our servers
  • All processing and analysis occur while your data remains encrypted
  • Only you possess the decryption key necessary to access your unencrypted health information

This means that even we, as the service provider, cannot see or access your actual health data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To maintain and improve the Service: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience

  • To ensure security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized activities
  • To comply with legal obligations: Meeting regulatory requirements and responding to legal requests
